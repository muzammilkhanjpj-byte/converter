Privacy Policy

Your security is our absolute priority. Learn how we handle your credential verification pipelines.

1. Information We Collect

Diginatize operates under extreme privacy structures. We only collect the minimal billing and verification credentials necessary to unlock downloads and process payments. This includes:

  • Your login credentials (email and security access keys).
  • Checkout transaction inputs processed via encrypted gateways.
  • System parameters and diagnostic logs for optimal delivery performance.

2. Processing & Security

All connection and transaction processes use military-grade HTTPS/TLS encryption. We do not store card details on our local database hubs. Credentials are parsed directly through secure third-party processing gateways so your vault is completely insulated.

3. Cookies & Session Storage

We use local browser session storage (such as localStorage) to maintain authorization state, secure purchase locks, and synchronize local collections across pages. This data resides solely on your client machine and is not mined or sold.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We never share, sell, distribute, or rent your personal information to marketing databases or unrelated third parties. Diginatize strictly operates to provide automated assets to our creative circle.

5. Updates and Access

Diginatize reserves the right to amend this privacy model. Continued use of our secure pipelines constitutes authorization of our parameters. If you have any inquiries regarding your private vault data, please contact the administrators.
